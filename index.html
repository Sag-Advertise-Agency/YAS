Building Visions, Creating Legacies

Who We Are

YAS Construction & Finishing, located in K.S.A. and Cairo, Egypt, has been active as a main contractor in construction, fine finishing, and technical support for corporate, commercial, and residential projects since 2016 and most recently hospitality. YAS has been growing rapidly with a diverse team of professionals; ranging from young talent to field experts with more than 14 years of experience. YAS has successfully delivered multiple high-quality projects in the market on time and prides itself on providing services that meet client expectations.

Our Mission

We are fully committed to delivering exceptional construction and comprehensive technical services to all our clients. Our goal is to not only meet but exceed expectations by consistently implementing the best practices. We aim to foster and maintain long-term partnerships grounded in trust, superior quality assurance, reliable service delivery, and the highest safety standards.
